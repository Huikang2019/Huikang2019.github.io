Research Interests

My research area is mainly in continuous optimization and its application to machine learning, signal processing, and data analysis. I am particularly interested in various analytic issues of optimization problems and algorithms arising from these applications.

Research Experience & Education

I joint the Department of Electronic Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong as an research associate, working with Professor Wing Ken Ma. I received my Ph.D. in 2018 from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Anthony Man-Cho So. Prior to that, I received my B.S. in Mathematics from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2014.
