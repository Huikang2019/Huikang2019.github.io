Research Interests

My research area is mainly in continuous optimization and its application to machine learning, statistics and signal processing. I am particularly interested in various analytic issues of optimization problems and algorithms arising from these applications.

Research Experience & Education

I received my BSE degree in Mathematics from University of Science and Technology of China in 2014. Then, I received my PhD degree in Department of Systems Engineering and Engineering Management from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. After that, I joint the Department of Electronic Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong as a research associate, working with Dr. Wing-Kin (Ken) Ma. Now, I am a research associate in the Business School at Imperial College London, under the supervison of Prof. Wolfram Wiesemann.
