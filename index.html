Research Interests

My research area is mainly in continuous optimization and its application to machine learning, signal processing, and data analysis. I am particularly interested in various analytic issues of optimization problems and algorithms arising from these applications.

Research Experience & Education

I received my B.S. in Mathematics from University of Science and Technology of China in 2014. Then, I received my Ph.D. in Department of System Engineering & Engineering Management from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Anthony Man-Cho So. After that, I joint the Department of Electronic Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong as an research associate, working with Dr. Wing-Kin (Ken) Ma.
